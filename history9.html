Vietnam War & U.S. Influence

Thailand became a crucial ally of the United States during the Vietnam War, allowing American forces to use its territory for military bases and operations. In exchange, Thailand received extensive economic and military aid, fueling modernization projects, infrastructure growth, and economic development. This era also saw increased cultural exchange, as American presence influenced Thai society.
