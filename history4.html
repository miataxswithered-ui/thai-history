Bangkok & Rama I

After the assassination of King Taksin, General Chakri ascended the throne as King Rama I in 1782, founding the Chakri dynasty, which still reigns today. Rama I moved the capital across the Chao Phraya River to a more defensible position and established Bangkok (Rattanakosin) as the new center of Thai politics and culture.
